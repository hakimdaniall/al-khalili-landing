About Us

Strengthening Malaysia's Education System

Al Khalili Consultant is a provider of seminars and expos in the education industry in Malaysia. With support from the Ministry of Education Malaysia (MOE), we began our operations in 2015 with the program 'KE ARAH MELAHIRKAN PELAJAR ALIRAN SAINS'.

We receive annual support letters from MOE acknowledging our role in strengthening the national education system. Each year, we offer seminars and workshops to nearly one million school students nationwide, particularly those preparing for public examinations.

We also organize Higher Education Expos and Roadshows in five major Malaysian cities to help school leavers prepare for higher education enrollment.

Mission

To unlock the potential of students academically by connecting them with subject matter experts.

Vision

To empower students to achieve more.

Objective

To share the experience and expertise of Guru Cemerlang (Excellent Teachers) from the Ministry of Education with all students across Malaysia, from primary to pre-university levels.

Products & Services

What We Offer

Seminars & Workshops

We connect students with a pool of Guru Cemerlang (over 2000 nationwide under MOE). These teachers are specialists in their fields and skilled in pedagogy and communication.

Each year, our seminars and workshops reach nearly 1 million students. The programs are focused on:

  • 'SCORE A Seminar' (Answering Techniques & Marking Schemes)
  • 'Higher Order Thinking Skills'

These programs help students excel in public examinations.

Higher Education Fair & Roadshow

An annual event designed to support students and school leavers with college preparation.

In conjunction with:

  • PGBK
  • College Readiness Seminar

The roadshows are held in Johor Bahru, Kuala Lumpur, Pulau Pinang, Kota Kinabalu, and Kuching after SPM and STPM examinations.

FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions

What educational services does Al Khalili Consultant provide?

We provide educational seminars and workshops for students preparing for public examinations, as well as Higher Education Expos and Roadshows in major Malaysian cities to help school leavers explore higher education opportunities.

How are your seminars different from regular school classes?

Our seminars are conducted by Guru Cemerlang (Excellent Teachers) recognized by the Ministry of Education. They provide specialized exam preparation techniques, focused content review, and practical strategies that complement regular classroom learning.

Do you offer programs for all education levels?

Yes, we offer programs for students from primary school through pre-university levels, with specialized focus on those preparing for major public examinations like PT3, SPM, and STPM.

How can schools arrange for seminars at their institutions?

Schools can contact us directly through our website or email to discuss arrangements for on-site seminars. We work closely with school administrators to tailor programs that meet the specific needs of their students.
